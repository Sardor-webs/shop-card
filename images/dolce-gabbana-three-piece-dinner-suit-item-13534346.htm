      Dolce & Gabbana three-piece dinner suit

      When it comes to formal occasions, nothing quite beats Italian tailoring and this dinner suit from Dolce & Gabbana ticks all the style boxes. Crafted from a blend of virgin wool and silk in slick black, this set is composed of three pieces. Firstly, the blazer jacket displays peaked notched lapels, a front button fastening, long sleeves with button cuffs, a chest and front flap pockets and a rear central vent, layering a v-neck waistcoat with a front button fastening, two front pockets and an adjustable backstrap. The slim fitted trousers feature a waistband with belt loops, a button and zip fly, side slit pockets, two back buttoned welt pockets, a regular length and avant-garde appliqué stripes at the sides.

      Made in Italy
